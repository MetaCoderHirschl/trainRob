Plot Summary

The clerk at the train station is assulted and left tied by four men.

Then they rob the train threatening the operator.

They take all the money an shoot a passenger when trying to run away.

little girl discovers the clerk tied and gives notice to the sherriff,

who at once goes along with his men hunting the bandits.

They catch up with the bandits, There is a shoot-out,

and one of the robbers gets killed.

The remaining robbers make a run for it by horse and get away with the loot.
